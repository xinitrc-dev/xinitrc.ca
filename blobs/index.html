About Us

We are a community server, that aims to provide nice and relaxing social space for all groups out there. Started out as an emoji server, we've grown to be a strong community and we welcome everyone to join us! Unlike many other servers, we try to keep it simple, avoiding multiple pages of rules, and many unnecessary channels! We separate all the channels into convenient categories, so you can mute and hide them at your convenience.

Emojis

We've started out as an emoji server, after Google has decided to move away from "blobs", in our server you will find many blob emojis, both regular and animated!

Community

Tired of complex rules and many useless text channels? Want simple relaxing experience? This server has few rules outside of Discord's ToS and all of the text channels are split into convenient categories, so you can easily hide and mute them!
